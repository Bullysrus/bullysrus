Cujo

About Cujo:

Cujo is an amazing example of a standard Fluffy French Bulldog. He features a teddy bear look, with big shoulders, no tail and a compact structure. His color and long (L4) Fluffy fur make him very unique. Cujo is a Proven stud and has produced multiple litters with 6-10 pups.

Health and Temperament:
Cujo has great breathing, is quite atheletic and very functional. He has the most loving, playful, and sweet temperament.
