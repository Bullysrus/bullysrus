Frequently Asked Questions

We know bringing home a new puppy is a big decision. Below are some commonly asked questions about our puppies, reservation process, and what you can expect when welcoming a Bullys R Us puppy into your family.

Our puppies are ready to go to their forever homes between 8-12 weeks old depending on their individual development.

Puppies are available to reserve starting at 4 weeks old. Reservations are accepted on a first-come, first-served basis with a deposit.

Each puppy goes home with their health record, age-appropriate vaccinations, deworming, and helpful information to make the transition into their new home easier.

Registration options depend on the puppy and placement agreement. AKC registration details will be discussed before reservation.
