Welcome To Bullys R Us

At Bullys R Us, we are dedicated to producing healthy, well-structured, and exceptional French Bulldogs with loving temperaments and great pedigrees. Bullys R Us began back in 2021 with our first American Bully, and have since fallen in love with French Bulldogs as well.
Every puppy is raised with love, socialized from an early age, and treated as part of our family to ensure they become confident, lifelong companions. Our pups are ensured to have their first vaccination before ever going to their new home. Pups will go home with a health record, optional AKC registraion, and a bag of their food for an easy diet transition.
Located in California's Central Valley, we are passionate about preserving and improving the breed through responsible breeding, health, structure, and temperament. Whether you're searching for your next family member or a quality addition to your breeding program, we're committed to matching every puppy with the perfect home and providing ongoing support every step of the way.
We offer shipping, pickup, delivery, as well as our stud services.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reserve a puppy?

Our puppies are reserved on a first-come, first-served basis. To reserve your puppy, a small deposit is required to secure your chosen companion. We accept secure payment options including but not limited to Zelle, Cashapp, and ApplePay. We welcome families to meet their puppy in person before making their reservation. Once a deposit has been placed, we will hold your puppy for up to two weeks while final arrangements are made. We are excited to help you find the perfect addition to your family and create a smooth, enjoyable experience from reservation to pickup.

What are your available delivery options?

We are happy to offer convenient delivery options for our Bullys R Us families.
Complimentary delivery is available within a 100-mile radius of Modesto, California. For families located out of state, we offer hand delivery throughout the United States for an additional fee starting at $400. Every puppy is transported with care and safety as our top priority. All puppy meetings and handoffs will take place in a secure public location to ensure a safe and comfortable experience for both our families and our puppies.

Are puppies fully vaccinated?

At Bullys R Us, the health and well-being of our puppies is our top priority. All puppies will go home with their first set of **DHPP vaccinations**, providing protection against **Parvovirus, Distemper, Adenovirus, and Parainfluenza**. Each puppy will also receive a detailed health record documenting their vaccinations, deworming treatments, and care history. This allows every family to have a clear understanding of their puppy’s health journey from the very beginning.
