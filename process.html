Our Puppy Process

At Bullys R Us, we are dedicated to raising healthy, happy, and well-socialized French Bulldogs. Our goal is to provide each family with a wonderful companion and a smooth experience from reservation to bringing your puppy home.

1. Apply For A Puppy

Begin by filling out our puppy application. This helps us learn about your lifestyle, experience, and what you are looking for in your future companion.

2. Meet Your Puppy

Families are welcome to meet available puppies in person before making their reservation.

3. Reserve Your Puppy

Puppies are reserved on a first-come, first-served basis. A deposit is required to secure your chosen puppy.

4. Puppy Care

All puppies go home with age-appropriate care, including their first DHPP vaccination, deworming treatments, and a health record documenting their care.

5. Bringing Your Puppy Home

We offer delivery options within our area and hand delivery throughout the United States. We want every puppy to transition into their new family safely and comfortably.
