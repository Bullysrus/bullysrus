Our Puppy Process

At Bullys R Us, we are dedicated to raising healthy, happy, and well-socialized French Bulldogs. Our goal is to provide each family with a wonderful companion and a smooth experience from reservation to bringing your puppy home.
Our pups are available to reserve starting at 4 weeks old, and ready to go home to their forever families at 8-12 weeks.

1. Apply For A Puppy

Begin by filling out our puppy application or by contacting us directly at any of our available messaging options.

2. Meet Your Puppy

Families are welcome to FaceTime videocall or meet available puppies in person before making their reservation.

3. Reserve Your Puppy

Pups can be reserved starting at 4 weeks old, and will be ready to go home once they reach 8 weeks old. Puppies are reserved on a first-come, first-served basis. A small deposit is required to secure your chosen puppy.

4. Puppy Care

All puppies go home with age-appropriate care, including their first DHPP vaccination, deworming treatments, and a health record documenting their care. If you purchase Full AKC registration with breeding rights, it will also be sent home with your pup.

5. Bringing Your Puppy Home

A small deposit is required for delivery. Families can also pickup here in Modesto. We offer low cost delivery options within California, and hand delivery anywhere in the United States. We offer complimentary delivery within 100 miles of Modesto. We want every puppy to transition into their new family safely and comfortably.
